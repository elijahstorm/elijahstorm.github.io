Can these Bees, without implicit instructions, manage to bring pollen to the hive and escape the Zombie Flies?

Bees uses Boids math to determine how it moves, and they cannot communicate.

For every 20 Pollen they collect, a Fly will disappear. And Butterflies will distract and stop a bee for a few seconds.

The bees' Ai will try to bring pollen to the hive while Zombie Flies try and kill the bees.

The AI will learn to maximize the pollen reward each run.
