Quarantine Reservation System

Created an online reservation booking system for a government mandated quarantine facility in South Korea. I used different REST APIs and managed user traffic and implemented sales through the website for the ultimate user experience. Successfully managing a high volume of customer's needs and designing the website to be easily accessible and understandable to foreigners entering into the country and needing to mandatorily quarantine.

Converting the users to the checkout page, and using PayPal to conduct sales through the website and send reservation data back to our internal systems.

Visual AI Admin Dashboard

Easy Ai Development Team Management System. Our idea was to make clean and easy software that will rival big companies in the manufacturing of Visual AI data. Our systems will allow team managers to easily program and edit AI Training data without the need of an expensive Engineer and Education fees.

Our AI is using Firebase for data storing and user-base management. the goal is to make this software able to software the one-stop management solution for companies to train their Visual AI systems without the complexity of the existing models. Hopefully people can use this technology to streamline their own development and creation in the world, so we can take advantage that the beauty that AI Systems can bring, without excluding who can do it to the Educated Engineers.

I learned a lot about how to manage the work-flow of huge data-focused projects, and seamless streaming of that live data across all platforms (APIs, mobile, iOS, android, web, etc). The most important thing to me was making the process of future work easier, because I knew a project of this scope could become massive very quickly. Making a scalable data management system that had a very high-level API use, to allow the developers who work after me an easy time developing and maintaining key features.

Arrival App (team of 10)
