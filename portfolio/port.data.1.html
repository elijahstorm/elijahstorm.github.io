Chess (AI)

 
 
 
 
 
Converting the users to the checkout page, and using PayPal to conduct sales through the website and send reservation data back to our internal systems.

Using iterative deepening and advanced pruning techniques, the AI engine currently picks what it thinks is the best move, while looking 6 moves ahead in 100ms (suprisingly impressive speeds); however, my evaluate function is not as accurate as I would like. This chess AI by no means boasts being the best in the world, and will most likely fall to an engine like Stockfish.
 
 

ThunderLite (HTML5 Game)

 
 
 
 
Coded entirely in HTML5, JavaScript and Node.js websockets, with the user-auth, and database running on MongoDB.

A live, online, multiplayer game that I created in 2018, based off of a slightly popular online game from the late 2000s that I played. I was always fascinated how the game worked and I wanted to recreate it. The original game has long since been abandoned by its creators, and the community fell apart. I thought it would be a fun and harmless project to recreate something I once found so much joy in!
