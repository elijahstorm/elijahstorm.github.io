Collabrative Photography (team of 2)

On a trip to Los Angeles in 2016, a high school friend of mine and I decided to do a collaborative project. We took many pictures and edited them, then found the best 12 and tied them to a musical project. One track represents one picture. This was something we had a lot of fun with, and kick-started my ambitions and desires to work on big projects moving forward into the future. I loved the satisfaction of seeing something come to life and working with my friends to achieve a common goal that would be impossible by myself.

We have physical copies of all the albums and artwork printed from CD Baby. Super fun!
